Akan Namer

Most people do know when their birthdays are, but some might not know what day of the week they were born.
Akan Namer is a web application that is going to take in your birthday and calculates the day of the week
you were born and then depending on your gender it gives you a corresponding Akan Name.
Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture.For girls;Sunday: Akosua,Monday: Adwoa,Tuesday: Abenaa,Wednesday: Akua,
Thursday: Yaa,Friday: Afua and Saturday: Ama. For boys;Sunday: Kwasi,Monday: Kwadwo,Tuesday: Kwabena, Wednesday: Kwaku,
Thursday: Yaw,Friday: Kofi,and Saturday: Kwame.
Fill out the form and get to know your Akan name!
