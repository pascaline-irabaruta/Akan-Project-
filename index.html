Akan Namer

Akan Namer is a web application that is going to take in your birthday and calculates the day of the week you were born and then depending on your gender it gives you a corresponding Akan Name.
Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture.

For girls;Sunday: Akosua,Monday: Adwoa,Tuesday: Abenaa,Wednesday: Akua,
Thursday: Yaa,Friday: Afua and Saturday: Ama.
For boys;Sunday: Kwasi,Monday: Kwadwo, Tuesday: Kwabena, Wednesday: Kwaku,
Thursday: Yaw,Friday: Kofi,and Saturday: Kwame.
